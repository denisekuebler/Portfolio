Food Use - Lebensmittel nutzen statt entsorgen

  • Project from the Module "Entwicklungsprojekt Interaktive Systeme" at the TH Köln
  • Add fruit and vegetables to your inventory via QR-Code
  • The app calculates expiration dates based on factors like the kind, storage and origin country. User is notified when a food item is about to expire.
  • The app detects the ideal storage for food items based on ethylene production and stock.
  • Users can offer food that they don't want to use anymore to other users
  • The app recommends food offers that match the users personal perferences, restrictions, mobility and location.

Design einer Studie zur Konzeption einer bildungsbegleitenden Anwendung für Kinder mit Down-Syndrom

  • Project from the module "Praxisprojekt" at the TH Köln
  • Preparation for the Bachelors Thesis
  • Research on education, media usage and daily life of children with Down-Syndrome
  • The goal was to obtain knowledge which will help with conceptualising an E-Learning application for children with Down-Syndrome
  • Research about the target audience, including psychological and physical abilities and restrictions
  • Design of a questionnaire targeted at the parents of children with Down-Syndrome
  • Design of an interview guide for personal interviews with the parents and teachers of the children
  • Conduction of Pre-Tests regarding the questionnaire and interview guides.
