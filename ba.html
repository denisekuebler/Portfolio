Auswertung einer explorativen Studie zu Nutzungskontexten in E-Learning Anwendungen fur Kinder mit Down-Syndrom und Identifikation relevanter Interaktions-Muster

Bachelor Thesis written during the Winter-Semester 2019/2020.

Project Overview

  • Evaluation of the questionnaire from the "Praxisprojekt" using the method Thematic Analysis by Braun&Clarke with the goal of analysis the usage context of E-Learning Applications for children with Down-Syndrome.
  • Presentation of the results in form of personae.
  • Research regarding Interaction-Patterns.
  • Analysis of behavioural patterns of children with Down-Syndrome.
  • Definition of requirements towards an E-Learning application.
  • Analysis of existing E-Learning applications for children-with Down-Syndrom regarding used Interaction-Patterns.
  • Identification and explanation of the most relevant Interaction-Patterns used.

Data Evaluation

  • Evaluation of the collected data with the goal of identifying the context of use of E-Learning Applications for children with Down-Syndrome.
  • Inductive approach to gain new knowledge without any pre-existing bias.
  • Deductive approach to answer predefined research questions in relation to the context of use.
  • Encoding of data in order to identify themes within it.
  • Search for themes within Codes.
  • Defining and describing themes within data.
  • Presentation of the identified context of use of E-Learning Applications including preferred devices, social and physical surroundings while using the applications, needs, experience levels and more.

Creation of Personae

  • Translating results of the survey into Personae.
  • Creation of 2 personae to represent different children with different abilities, experience levels with media, goals, problems and everyday lives.
  • Despite there being many more than just 2 different kinds of users within the group of "Children with Down-Syndrom", the short time of the thesis didn't allow me to create more Personae. The two created personae represent two people with very different characteristics and needs in an attempt to at least look at applications from two entirely different yet relevant perspectives.

E-Learning for children with Down-Syndrome

  • Research on different kinds of E-Learning.
  • Discussing advantages and disadvantages of E-Learning for children with Down-Syndrome.
  • Market analysis on existing E-Learning applications specifically for the target group "Down-Syndrome".
  • Defining requirements for E-Learning applications based on the results of the conducted study.
  • Identifying Interaction-Patterns that could be used for a good user experience.

Identification of Interaction-Patterns

  • Testing existing E-Learning applications for children with Down-Syndrome.
  • Documenting workflows and the design of the UI by referencing Interaction-Patterns.
  • Evaluating used patterns under consideration of the childrens needs.
  • Documenting and explaining recurring patterns and their implementation.
  • Evaluation of used patterns vs. patterns that should have been used based on the context of use.
